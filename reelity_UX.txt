# 🌍 1. CORE PURPOSE (VERY IMPORTANT)

Reelity is NOT just a feed.

It is:

> A **Cultural + Commerce + Community Feed Engine**
> where social interaction directly connects with economy (FCU, Merch, Auctions, Clubs)

As defined in system:

* It connects users, brands, collectibles, auctions, and updates into one unified stream
