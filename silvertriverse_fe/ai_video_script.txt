GLOBAL VISUAL STYLE GUIDE (Apply to Every Scene)
--------------------------------------------------
COLOR PALETTE:
  • Background    : Deep navy / midnight blue (#070d1f, #0a1128)
  • Accent Gold   : Warm amber gold (#f59e0b, #fcd34d) — glows on hover/select
  • Text Primary  : Pure white (#ffffff)
  • Text Secondary: Cold gray (#94a3b8)
  • Glow Purple   : Violet (#a78bfa, #7c3aed) — used for Land / collectibles
  • Teal          : #22d3ee — rare items, verified badges
  • Success Green : #4ade80 — confirmations, votes

TYPOGRAPHY STYLE:
  • Headings : Serif font (Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond style)
              — Large, bold, letter-spaced, white with faint text-shadow glow
  • Body     : Clean sans-serif (Inter style), gray, small tracking
  • Labels   : ALL-CAPS, micro tracking (0.2em–0.4em), gold or teal tint

UI ELEMENT STYLE:
  • Cards     : Rounded corners (16px), semi-transparent dark glass surface
               — border 1px partially opaque (border-navy, border-gold/30)
               — subtle inner shadow or gradient overlay
  • Buttons   : Gold fill with dark text for primary
               — Ghost/outline (border gold, gold text) for secondary
  • Glow FX   : Items glow with their colour on hover (amber, purple, teal)
  • Animations: Smooth, spring-based — elements fade+slide up into view

CAMERA / MOTION NOTES:
  • Prefer slow, smooth camera glides — no jarky cuts
  • Use shallow depth-of-field on close-up UI elements
  • Transitions: Dissolve cross-fade or fast cinematic wipe (left to right)
  • Lighting: Imagine a dark studio with coloured LED ambient lighting
              matching each section's accent colour

CHARACTERS ON SCREEN:
  Use stylised digital avatars or silhouettes, not real people.
  The PRIMARY USER is a young creative woman — dark studio setting,
  looking at a glowing ultra-wide monitor showing the app interface.
  She has tasteful creative attire (dark tones, minimal gold jewellery).
