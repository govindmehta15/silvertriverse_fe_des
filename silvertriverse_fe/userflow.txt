OVERVIEW
--------
SilverTriverse is an entertainment super-app that blends social discovery,
collectibles, AI-powered creative tools, and a virtual land economy — all
wrapped in a cinematic, dark-luxury UI.

Target Audience  : Film fans, collectors, independent creators, studio professionals
Tone of Video    : Premium, aspirational, fast-paced — like a movie trailer
Total Duration   : ~5 minutes
Recommended      : Screen recording at 1440p, smooth mouse movements, no typing errors
Background Music : Cinematic/electronic, low-key during narration, swells at reveals
